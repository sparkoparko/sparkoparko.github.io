Storytelling through social media

Sharing about the Asian American experience

Overview

Throughout college, I was surrounded by a strong community of Asian Americans who encouraged me to embrace my ethnic identity. I wanted to share about the Asian American experience so my followers could also learn, heal, and grow to embrace their own. I introduced topics such as failure, belonging, and mental health through Instagram.

Context

6 weeks long

Personal project

Role

Storytelling

Understanding my Instagram users

The Research

I wanted to interact and influence a close group of friends, rather than people who are interested in the topic. I have a private account that allows me to selectively choose who consumes my content, so I decided Instagram was my ideal storytelling medium over Facebook. Instagram is also the 2nd most used social platform after Facebook, yet generates 4x more interactions through its Instagram Storys.

The demographic

A few interesting insights:

  • 90% are Asian American
  • 67% identify as women
  • 67% are friends from college - but 51% are from one student organization
