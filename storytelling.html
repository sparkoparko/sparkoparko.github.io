Home: a series

Overview

I'm slowly preparing to transition from college to work. As I reflect on what it means to belong somewhere and my growth, I decided to explore the idea of 'home' through social media. I told my experience that shapes my idea of home, gathered people's individual stories, and shared how displacement and belonging is a collective experience.

Understanding my Instagram users

The Research

I wanted to interact and influence a close group of friends, rather than people who are interested in the topic. I have a private account that allows me to selectively choose who consumes my content, so I decided Instagram was my ideal storytelling medium over Facebook. Instagram is also the 2nd most used social platform after Facebook, yet generates 4x more interactions through its Storys.
