I'm Sarah Park from Seattle.

I'm a fourth year student transitioning from studying at the University of Washington. I will be graduating in Spring 2020 and am looking for new opportunities.

My college experience was nonlinear as I explored different disciplines to answer, “How can I improve the welfare of others?”. I've discovered that technology is a powerful tool and can address pressing issues in our society.

As someone that strives to create the best experience for users, I advocate for disadvantaged users and marginalized groups throughout the design process. Systematic inequality favors the more privileged groups. Within this context, I aim to be inclusive, thoughtful, and empathetic.
