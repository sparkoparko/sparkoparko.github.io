Overview

The Associated Students of the University of Washington (ASUW) engages the campus community through programming, services and advocacy. The Student Disability Commission (SDC), one of its 25 entities, advocates for undergraduate students with disabilities. #DistanceLearningNow was passed as a bill through the student senate in support of remote, video-recorded lectures in all classes.
